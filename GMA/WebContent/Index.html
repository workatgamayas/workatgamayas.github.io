ABOUT THE ACADEMY

Ghantasala Music Academy

Ghantasala Music Academy started in the year 1997 by GT Srinivas Rao who is renowned artist. Revered SPB has led the Academy as President along with other well-liked music artists. Having done 100+ live events, Ghantasala Music Academy has staged various prominent artists like M. Balamuralikrishna, S.P. Balasubrahmanyam, Saluri Koteswara Rao, Susheela,and many others. Famous events include Mega Music Gala by Koti, Swarabhishekem: Has helped multiple singers and artists of current decade. We have also started a Music School in Khamam in the year 1999, inaugurated by SPB to promote the cultural studies and upbringing talent of music enthusiasts.
