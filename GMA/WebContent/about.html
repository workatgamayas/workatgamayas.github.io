Brief of GMA Academy

In our all human generations, music has played an important role in the lifestyle of humans. Here Significant evidence has been discovered that very early man developed primitive flute from animal bones.


Voice would have been the first expression of music in our distant ancestors used to bond comfort a sleepless child. These humble beginnings, the music has evolved in all generations to enjoy today.


GMA has started in 1997 at Khammam by G.T.Srinivas and other members. Revered sri.S.P.Balasubrahmanyam has lead GMA as Honorary President and himself conducted "little musicians show" as host at GMA platform at Khammam. Having done 100+ live events on stages with prominent Smt. Suseela, Smt.Chitra., Sri.S.P.Balu., Sri.V.Ramakrishna., Sri.Koti.& Sri Raj and Sri.Akkineni Nageswarao, Sri.Kantharao, Sri.Ramana Murthy Sri.Mikkilineni.,Sri Rajendra Prasad, Sri.RaghuBabu and Daggubati Suresh., Sri.Chota.K.Naidu and Sri.Muppalaneni Shiva.


GMA is a leader in organising and conducting musical programs to inspire music-loving people. GMA is committed to the highest standards of musicianship by working with legendary stars of music to provide quality in a professional and healthy environment and to develop the culture & discipline in people.
